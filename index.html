About Us

Grassroots India is a Section 8 not-for-profit Company dedicated to charitable activities and holistic community development. We work tirelessly to uplift the vulnerable by providing emergency relief, quality education, healthcare services, and sustainable livelihood opportunities. Our commitment is to create lasting positive change in the lives of those who need it most, fostering a more equitable and compassionate society.

Our Mission

To undertake charitable activities for the development of the vulnerable.

Our Vision

To promote education, arts, and science at all levels.

Our Approach

To provide emergency relief, basic care, and public health services.

Our Core Pillars of Work

Relief & Rehabilitation

Providing immediate emergency relief and long-term rehabilitation support to communities affected by natural disasters and crises.

Education For All

Ensuring access to quality education for underprivileged children and promoting lifelong learning opportunities across all age groups.

Health & Nutrition

Delivering essential healthcare services and nutritional support to improve the well-being of vulnerable populations.

Women Empowerment

Empowering women through skill development, economic opportunities, and advocacy for their rights and dignity.

Livelihood & Commerce

Creating sustainable livelihood opportunities and supporting small-scale commerce to promote economic independence.

Peace & Harmony

Fostering interfaith dialogue, cultural understanding, and community harmony to build a more peaceful society.
